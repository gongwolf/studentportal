Short Essay

The purpose of this essay is to tell us how you got to where you are now in college, and what you plan to do in the future, academically and professionally. This component of the application must be written thoughtfully and carefully, as it will be heavily weighted in whether or not you will be selected. This must be written with several paragraphs as an essay with an introduction and Conclusion. Each paragraph should develop the topic sentence of the paragraph very well, responding to each of the prompts below. The content of your essay must be substantially thought out, so please provide enough time, thought, and effort to this very important part of the application, or your chances of selection will be reduced.

Please address the following prompts:

  1. Recount a significant experience that influenced you to decide you wanted a college degree. (400 words remaining)



  2. What semester/year do you plan to transfer to a four-year university, and to which institution do think you will transfer? (400 words remaining)



  3. What do you plan to do when you finish your B.S. degree? (Consider options: Graduate school to earn an M.S. or Ph.D.? Specific type of profession? Specific company/organization, or workplace? Specific passion to do something you have always wanted to do?) (400 words remaining)



  4. How do you think this Professional Development Workshop will help you reach your goals, and how else could New Mexico AMP assist you? (400 words remaining)
